# Amino Group

## TL;DR:
[[Functional group]] $-NHH$ and important [[Organic Compound|organic]] [[base]].

## Definition:
![[Pasted image 20220620150924.png]][^1]

They are categorized into 3 groups:
- [[Primary Amine]]
- [[Secondary Amine]]
- [[Tertiary Amine]]

Amino Groups are mainly protonated and positively charged at pH 7.
