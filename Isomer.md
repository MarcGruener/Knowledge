# Isomer

## TL;DR:
Molecules with an identical molecular formulae (same number of atoms of each element) but a distinct arrangement of the atoms in space.[^1]

## Definition:
There are three types of Isomers:
- [[Positional Isomer]]
- [[Geometric Isomer]]
- [[Optical Isomer]]
