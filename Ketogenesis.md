# Ketogenesis

## TL;DR:
The process of breaking down fatty acids and producing [[Ketone]].[^1]

## Definition:
*"The liver forms [[Ketone|ketone bodies]] from fatty acids during fasting but not from [[Carbohydrate|carbohydrates]] after meal."*[^2]
