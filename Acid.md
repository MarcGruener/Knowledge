# Acid

## TL;DR:
Substance that releases a proton (positive charge).[^1]

## Definition:
More $H^+$ than $OH^-$.[^3]

Molecule or [[ion]] capable of either donating a proton ([[Brønsted–Lowry acid]]) or forming a covalent bond with an electron pair ([[Lewis Acid]]).[^2]
