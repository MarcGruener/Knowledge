# Peptide bond

## TL;DR:
[[Covalent bond]] between the $C1$ of one [[Amino Acids|amino acid]] and $N2$ of another [[Amino Acids|amino acid]] of a [[Amino Acids|peptide]] formed through [[dehydration]].[^1]
